About

Ella Patras is a passionate musical theater performer currently pursuing her Bachelor of Music in Music Theater at the Oberlin Conservatory of Music. With over 10 years of training in voice, acting, and dance, Ella brings versatility and dedication to every role she takes on.

Her recent achievements include winning First Place in the 2024 Oscar Hammerstein International Solo Competition (College Division) and being named a Songbook Academy Finalist. Ella has performed in professional, regional, community, and college productions, showcasing her range from leading roles like Wendla in Spring Awakening to ensemble work in beloved classics.
